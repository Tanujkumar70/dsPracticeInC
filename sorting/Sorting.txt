*selection sort*
    phle minimum find kr fir usko aage chipka de. 
    repeat;;;

*bubble sort*
    line me ja element ko uske next vale se check kr.
    agar bda aaye to swap kr.
    esse tujhe last me jo sbse bda hoga vo milega..
    repeat;;;

*insertion sort*
    do me divide kr sorted and unsorted 
    intially sorted me kuch nhi hoga sare unsorted me honge
    unsorted me se ek utha usko sorted me shi position pe daal jaise tera maannn ho vaise;
    repeat;;;
